Our Story

Shree Jaipur Fabs

Shree Jaipur Fabs is a celebrated clothing boutique nestled in the heart of Jaipur's vibrant Brahampuri neighborhood. We specialize in authentic Rajasthani block-printed garments, offering an exquisite range of dresses, shirts, pajamas, cotton sarongs, bags, and fabrics — each piece a testament to the rich artisanal heritage of Rajasthan. With a perfect 5-star rating from our valued customers, we take pride in delivering exceptional quality, vibrant colors, and unique designs that speak to both traditional and contemporary tastes.

Whether you are a local shopper or a traveler seeking a genuine piece of Jaipur's craft culture, Shree Jaipur Fabs welcomes you with warm hospitality and a carefully curated collection. Our team is known for being friendly, professional, and deeply passionate about the craft they represent. Open six days a week from 10 AM to 8 PM, we invite you to step in, explore, and take home a piece of Rajasthan's artistic soul.

  • Perfect 5-Star Rating — Trusted and loved by customers from across India and around the world
  • Authentic Rajasthani Block Printing — Every piece is handcrafted using traditional techniques passed down through generations
  • Wide Variety Under One Roof — From dresses and shirts to bags and raw fabrics, find everything you need in one friendly, welcoming store
